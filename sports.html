Football

Football image
Football is a very popular sport played all around the world. It is played between two teams, and each team has 11 players on the field. One player in each team is the goalkeeper, also called the goalie, whose main job is to stop the ball from entering the goal. The goalkeeper protects the net and is the only player allowed to use their hands inside the penalty area. Players pass, dribble, and kick the ball to score goals against the opposing team. A football match usually lasts for 90 minutes and requires teamwork, speed, and skill. The sport is governed internationally by FIFA, which organizes the biggest football tournament, the FIFA World Cup. Famous footballers like Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo have inspired millions of fans around the world. ⚽🥅
